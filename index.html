FAQ
What is the theme of the Hackathon?
We at CodeUtsava admire to assist our geeks to explore new domains, hence there isn’t any specific theme. However, once you have been shortlisted, we’ll let you know about the problem statements via the registered email ids.
What will be provided during the Hackathon?
CodeUtsava stands out among all hackathons for its hospitality. So gear yourselves up for some tasty, yummy food, exciting goodies, fun activities and ecstatic selfie booths to make your hacking experience a memorable one.
On what basis will the teams be shortlisted?
Your resume says it all!! Hence, teams would be shortlisted on the basis of resume and Git-Hub profiles of the team members as uploaded at the time of registration.
What should be the team size?
Teams must strictly consist of 3 members.
What about accommodation and travel reimbursement?
All the participants will be provided accomodation from 15th February,19 (morning) till 17th February,19 (evening) at our campus. However, we would never want to lose out our developer due to such reasons. If you need any more help, do send us a mail and we’ll try to help you out in every way possible.
Is there any specific eligibility criteria for the participants?
Age is no bar for our developers at CodeUtsava. All that matters to us is the zeal to code and develop. Whether you are a college student, a startup enthusiast, a working professional, we would love to have you on board.
Is there any prize for girl participants?
The answer is a big “yes”. A seperate prize has been kept to increase the partcipation of female coders in our Hackathon.
How to reach NIT Raipur?
Raipur, capital of Chhattisgarh is well connected to all metro cities via railways as well flights. Further, regular auto-rickshaws, uber, and Ola commute between station/airport to our college.
